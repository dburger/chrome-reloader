Chrome Reloader Options

Refresh interval is the interval between each reload. Wobble defines how to vary the refresh interval. A random value is subtracted from the interval from 0 up to the wobble value. For example, an interval of 300 with a wobble of 60 would mean that the site would be reloaded randomly at intervals anywhere from 4 to 5 minutes. A wobble of 0 means that the interval is strictly followed with no variation.
